Amrita CyberNation (ACN) 2022

About

Cybernation is a tech fest and envisions providing a platform for faculty members, research scholars, students,
and industry experts who are passionate about cybersecurity to unite and share their innovative ideas while learning about
how technology can be integrated with myriad other fields. It is intended to provide collaboration initiatives among universities
and industries. Amrita CyberNation will be a platform for initiating discussions on challenging aspects of Cyber Security for our Nation.
Students will also get an opportunity to interact and participate in many challenges in Cyber Domain where participants are from throughout the nation.
